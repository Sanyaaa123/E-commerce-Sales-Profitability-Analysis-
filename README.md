**E-Commerce Sales & Profitability Analysis**

Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales data to identify revenue trends, profitability patterns, and key business drivers.

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes an e-commerce transaction dataset to evaluate sales performance, profit margins, and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard helps identify high-performing product categories, regional sales trends, and loss-making segments to support data-driven business decisions.

**Tools & Technologies**

Power BI

SQL

Python

Data Cleaning & Transformation

Data Visualization

**Key Metrics**

Total Sales: 2.30M

Total Profit: 286.4K

Total Orders: 5K

Average Order Value (AOV): 458.61

**Key Insights**

Technology category generated the highest overall sales revenue.

Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.

The West region showed the highest sales performance compared to other regions.

Certain sub-categories such as Tables showed consistent losses, highlighting opportunities for pricing or cost optimization.

**Dashboard Features**

Sales and profit analysis by month, region, and year

Category and sub-category level profitability insights

Customer segment performance comparison

KPI cards highlighting major business metrics

Interactive filters for dynamic data exploration
